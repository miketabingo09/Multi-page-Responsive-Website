3 Month Plan

Short-term music lesson coverage usually involves focusing on a specific area of study for a short period of time. For example, a short-term lesson plan may focus on a particular technique, such as fingerpicking on the guitar, or a specific piece of music, such as a classical sonata. Short-term music lesson coverage may be helpful for students who want to refine a specific skill or prepare for a performance.

6 Month Plan

Medium-term music lesson coverage involves a broader focus, typically spanning several weeks or months. Medium-term lesson plans may include multiple techniques or pieces of music, and may incorporate a variety of musical genres. Medium-term music lesson coverage may be helpful for students who want to build a strong foundation in their instrument or explore different musical styles.

1 Year Plan

Long-term music lesson coverage is designed for students who want to develop a deep understanding of their instrument and achieve mastery over a long period of time. Long-term lesson plans may span several years or more, and may cover a wide range of topics, from technique and theory to performance and composition. Long-term music lesson coverage may be helpful for students who want to pursue a career in music or become professional musicians.
